Rainbow Hospital & Test-Tube Baby Centre In Bilaspur, Chhattisgarh Is One Of The Leading Health Care Providers In Field And Urology, Obs & Gynae And Infertility Problems.

Who We Are

Among The Finest Hospital In The City, Rainbow Hospital & Test-Tube Baby Centre In Tilak Nagar, Bilaspur, Chhattisgarh Is Known For Offering Excellent Patient Care. The Clinic Is Located Centrally In Tilak Nagar, A Prominent Locality In The City. It Stands Close To Main Post Office Which Not Only Makes It Convenient For People From The Vicinity To Consult The Doctor But Also For Those From Other Neighbourhood To Seek Medical Guidance. There Is No Dearth Of Public Modes Of Transport To Reach The Clinic From All Major Areas Of The City.
