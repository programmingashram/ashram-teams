More About Sai Baba Hospital.

Complete diabetes management including GTT, HbA1c analysis, Continuous blood glucose monitoring, Diabetic foot care and colour doppler, Diabetic nephropathy, diet and exercise counseling along with ICU admission facilities for Diabetic ketoacidosis and hypoglycemia management.

Continuous glucose monitoring system is novel system of blood sugar monitoring for continuous 14 days through an implanted chip.Patients with uncontrolled blood sugar levels and labile diabetes are benefited by it .

Blood pressure management including continuous blood pressure monitoring and management of stroke and heart attack.

Complete cardiac work up including serum markers , ECG ,treadmill test, 2D- Echocardiography, X Ray and defibrillator facility.

Well equipped ICU with central oxygen supply, Multipara monitors , ventilators ,Defibrillators well trained staff for management of cardiac , kidney , neurological , head injury, trauma , poisonings, snake bite and various of emergencies

Well equipped operation theatre with facilities for Laproscopic and endoscopic surgeries i,e. Gall stone removal, Thoracoscopy, Urethral stricture dilatation, Ureteric stone removal , Renal calculus removal through PCNL procedure, TURP for prostate.

C-Arm image intensifier for all kinds of Orthopaedic and renal surgeries.

General surgeries including Hernia , Hydrocoel , Piles , appendicitis etc.

Gynaecological surgeries including ovarian cyst , ovarian cancers ,Hyterectomy, Tubectomy and recanalization.

Neuro surgeries and spine surgeries.

Maxillofacial surgeries.

ENT surgeries.

Hospital has pain clinic for Chronic pain syndrome patientss.

Hospital has indoor facilities for EMERGENCY admission, lab investigations, X Ray and sonography , Ambulance and 24 by 7 Pharmacy.
